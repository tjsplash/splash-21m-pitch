Pick a team. Pick a winner. Survive.

How it works · FAQ

What's the prize pool and how is it structured?

$21,000,000 — guaranteed. The largest survivor prize pool ever assembled. Full payout structure is published in the official contest rules; the top of the board takes home a generational sum.

How much is it to enter and how many entries can I have?

Each entry is $1,000. A single verified player can submit up to 150 entries — a maximum investment of $150,000. Use our bulk-entry tool to upload and manage them in one dashboard.

Where can I play from?

40 US states plus Canada. You can submit picks from anywhere you're eligible — phone, tablet, or laptop. No flights. No proxies.

How is this different from Circa Survivor?

Circa caps entries at 10 per person, requires you to fly to Las Vegas, and forces players to pay Vegas-based proxies up to 30% of their entry fee to submit picks each week. Splash is remote, transparent, and proxy-free — and ours is bigger.

How does the marketplace work?

Once entries lock, your entries become tradeable assets. List them on the Splash Marketplace at any time during the season — sell whole, sell fractional, hedge, or double down on someone else's run. Market pricing reflects survival probability in real time.

What's Polymarket's role?

Polymarket is our prediction markets partner — DCM and DCO — and the official sponsor of the $21M contest. Splash is launching as an introducing broker on Polymarket later this year, and the two platforms are co-marketing the contest from kickoff through Super Bowl.

When are entries open?

Entries are open now. The contest locks at first kickoff, 1:00 PM ET on Sunday, September 13, 2026.
